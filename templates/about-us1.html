About Us

We strive to make every step we take, a testament of diversity, inclusivity and

empowerment.

Signify Me, as a community, is dedicated to advancing the inclusivity of individuals who are hard of hearing, providing a platform that facilitates expression of their identity, values, and cultural heritage. Through our efforts, we aim to foster a more inclusive society where everyone has the opportunity to communicate effectively and participate fully in community life.
Here are the four guidelines we stick to protect you in these shifting environments

We believe in uplifting one another.

We recognise that not everyone starts with the same opportunities in life and hence, foster an environment where no one feels excluded or marginalised. As a community, we come together to empower and understand each other, where we give and receive love unconditionally.

We give back to the community.

We find great joy in giving back to the community that has supported us by curating online resources and making them readily accessible to our users. Our aim is to enhance their knowledge and support them in exploring new horizons with ease.

We thrive on our creativity.

We enjoy constantly devising fresh and innovative solutions to tackle both overlooked challenges and those that push the boundaries of our abilities. We relish the opportunity to confront problems that have been underexplored or overlooked, seeing them as fertile ground for inventive approaches.

We trust our determination.

We are resolutely focused on raising awareness about the rights and needs of the deaf community, particularly in the realm of career and job opportunities. Our determination drives us to advocate tirelessly for equal access and inclusion in professional settings.
